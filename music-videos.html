Types of Music Videos

Narrative Music Videos

Tell a story to your song, with a narrative music video. We are Irish filmmakers with a background of film, so we know how to tell creative stories in a cinematic way.

Performance Music Videos

Nothing beats a live music performance, but the closest thing might be a live performance video. We can film your performance in a stylish, classy, and safe way, ideal for reaching out to your listeners in a socially distanced way, during the Coronavirus pandemic. We uphold the highest standards of Covid safety, and shoot with small crews on socially distanced sets. Live streamed music videos are also possible. Read more here.

Lyric Videos

Put all the focus on the lyrics, with a lyric video. There's a lot of creative options out there. Motion graphics based videos with just titles, or lyrics laid over cinematic footage. Get in touch, and we'd love to create a unique video for your and your song. Learn more about our animated videos here.
