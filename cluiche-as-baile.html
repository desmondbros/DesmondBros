Cluiche As Baile

Feature Documentary, TV, 2023

Synopsis: “Cluiche As Baile”, or “The Away Game”, meets the small but mighty GAA clubs in cities across Europe. Living abroad, separated by language, background, and geography, but connected by a single passion for Gaelic games. We meet a diverse group of people, from Ireland and across the world, as they make friends, learn a new sport, and battle for the trophy. Narrated by Síle Seoige.

Directed and produced by Cian Desmond and Jack Desmond

Commissioned by TG4.

Production company: Desmond Bros Media
