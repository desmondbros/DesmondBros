Speak to Her

Short Film, 2019

Obafemi is an Eritrean bouncer in a Dublin nightclub. He tries to work up the courage to speak to Natka, a bathroom attendant, and decides to learn Polish to reach across the language divide.

Winner, Best Short Form - Royal Television Society awards ROI 2019. Winner, Best Writing, RTS awards UK & ROI 2019.

Dir. by Jack Desmond. Produced by Philip Emo. Edited by Cian Desmond.
